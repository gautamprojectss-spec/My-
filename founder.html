Meet Isha

Love what you do.

Isha Poddar is a Graphic Design major with a Bachelor's in Multimedia (2014–17) from St. Xavier's, Kolkata. She graduated with a pocket full of hopes — and Colours & Patterns, a college branding project that came to fruition because she stuck to one mantra: love what you do. She places a high value on time and dedication, is a firm believer in karma, and is a happy working wife who enjoys travelling, watching movies, and eating delicious food.
